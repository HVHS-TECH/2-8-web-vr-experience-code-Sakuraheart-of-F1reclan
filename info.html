Deep within the lush tropical forests of the small Japanese island of Iriomote, lives a rare feline...
The Iriomote cat, a small leopard cat, occupies the smallest territory of any wild cat. It is critically endangered, with an estimated 100 individuals left. The following resources are recommended if you want to know more about this elusive animal, or donate to conservation initiatives.

  • Felidae Fund
  • Feline Conservation
  • Japan Tiger Elephant Fund
  • Iriomote Wildlife Conservation Center
